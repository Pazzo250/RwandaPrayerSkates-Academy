Fraud Disclaimer

Please read this Fraud Disclaimer carefully before using our website or services.

Protect Yourself from Fraudulent Activities: We are committed to providing a safe and secure online environment. However, it is important for you to be aware of the potential for fraudulent activities and to take precautions to protect yourself.

We Will Never Ask For:

  • Your password via email or phone.
  • Your full credit card details or bank account information through unsolicited communications.
  • Payments through unofficial channels or to unverified individuals.

Be Aware Of:

  • Phishing Emails: Fraudulent emails that may appear to be from us asking for sensitive information. Always verify the sender's email address and be cautious of suspicious links.
  • Scam Calls: Individuals impersonating our representatives asking for personal or financial details over the phone. Do not provide such information.
  • Fake Websites: Imitation websites designed to look like ours to steal your login credentials or payment information. Always ensure you are on our official website.
  • Social Media Scams: Fraudulent posts or messages on social media platforms promising unrealistic offers or asking for personal information.

If You Suspect Fraud:

If you receive any suspicious communication that appears to be from us, or if you believe you have been a victim of fraud, please take the following steps immediately:

  • Do not respond to the suspicious communication.
  • Do not click on any links or open any attachments.
  • Contact our official support team through the contact information provided on our official website.
  • If you have provided financial information, contact your bank or credit card company immediately.
  • Consider reporting the incident to your local law enforcement agency.

Disclaimer: We are not responsible for any losses or damages incurred as a result of fraudulent activities carried out by third parties. It is your responsibility to exercise caution and protect your personal information.
